Why should I test soil?

Soil testing helps identify nutrient imbalances, pH issues, and moisture levels, enabling you to make informed decisions for healthier crops and higher yields.

check_circle
What is soil classification?

Soil classification involves categorizing soil based on its properties, such as texture, composition, and fertility, to better understand its suitability for various crops.

check_circle
How does IoT benefit agriculture?

IoT devices provide real-time data, improving decision-making for farmers. By continuously monitoring soil and environmental conditions, farmers can optimize crop care and enhance sustainability.
